PRE DEPLOYMENT


All services at AAFS are built on the cornerstones of compassion and respect, and are aimed at fostering wellness. Although the conditions we address often feature common characteristics and behavioral patterns, we recognize that each client's journey is unique, and tailor treatment to suit individual needs.

AAFS is incorporated as a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization and is licensed under State of Minnesota Rule 31 to provide outpatient chemical dependency treatment to adults, adolescents, clients with children, and clients with chemical abuse or dependency and mental health disorders. AAFS is also licensed under State of Minnesota Rule 29 as a Community Mental Health Clinic. AAFS complies with Minnesota Rule 29 and Rule 31 regulations.
