TESTIMONIALS


What's the most important thing you got out of treatment?

"Staying sober, better communication, being able to trust my counselor, being alumni and being welcomed back if I need to go somewhere."
-Jackie M.

"Understanding of what people go through, values go to the back of your mind, loss of family friends all the pain you put on yourself, brought me back where I can control my decisions again, doing things that I wouldn’t do but make better decisions now I think before I react, I better behavior more discipline."
-Vincent W.

"I learned I don’t have to own other people’s problems, I don’t have to be subject to their opinions or feeling, I learned to respect and respect and meet them where they are, how to deal with anger through the process and thinking about it before I respond."
-Ricardo R.

"Knowing my inventory, meeting and making good friends the staff and keeping me structured is the main thing."
-Jerry G.

"Got a lot of tools to stay clean, the information I received about staying clean, parenting skills and the connection culturally. Prioritizing my daily journey has been refreshed, helped me be aware of skills I have but I didn’t know I had; writing skills and comprehension I had problems with certain things I read. Now I can work on those skills and there is much more to add."
-Marilyn G.
