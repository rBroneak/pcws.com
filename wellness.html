OUR WELLNESS SERVICES


The CEO provides tangible tools and practices designed to bridge the cultural gap between clients and helping professionals, and to assist organizations in the adoption of culturally competent organizational policies and practices. The goal of the CEO is to provide high quality cultural responsiveness and cultural sensitivity training and educational materials for organizations and individuals working with multi-cultural and ethnic populations across communities.

Training Resources
AAFS offers presentations and training tailored to organizational needs and offered as day-long seminars. Topics include:

  • Working with African American women and the wellness process
  • Culture and the impacts of chemical abuse
  • Advocacy, management and therapy
  • Women, aggression and systemic analysis
  • Building Practitioner Cultural Competence
